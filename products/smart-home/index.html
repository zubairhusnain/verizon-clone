Smart Home

The best smart home devices

Smart home refers to a home equipped with lighting, heating and cooling, and electronic devices that can be controlled remotely from your computer or cell phone. If you're interested in smart home devices and technology, you may be wondering where to start, or how to integrate your devices into one true smart home ecosystem.

To begin, you can start by narrowing down a brand you'd like to base your home environment around. If you're interested in using Amazon's Alexa, for example, you might want to shop for other smart home tech from Amazon, including Echo devices that can control your whole home hub, Amazon streaming devices, and beyond. Additionally, some products, like Ring doorbells, can work with a variety of brands and will integrate seamlessly with whichever products you choose.

Google products are also a great place to start when integrating your home's technology, with home hubs, speakers, and cameras. Control your home's thermostat, keep an eye on your family or pets with high tech cameras, or make smart reminders with Google's suite of smart home products.

Smart home security for peace of mind

If you're looking for efficient, innovative ways to protect your home, smart home security devices can be a huge help. Security cameras, smart doorbells, and other new devices make protecting your home a breeze, even when you're not there.

Doorbells with cameras that sync with your cell phone, for example, can help you see who's at your door (even if you're far from home). Other cameras can be installed and integrated into your smart home ecosystem as well, giving you a wide coverage area of your home both indoors and outside.

If you're ready to start building your smart home ecosystem, you can shop our wide selection of security cameras, smart home hubs, and more any time at Verizon. And, if you're looking to save money, you can browse our product deals to get new connected tech and accessories at a great price.
