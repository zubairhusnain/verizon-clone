100 GB Mobile Hotspot

​ Enjoy more data to share with
devices like tablets and laptops
for
only $10/mo. Your smartphone or tablet can be used as a Wi-Fi connection, providing 100 GB of data for devices such as tablets, laptops and more. The 100 GB will be added to your plan allowance. After your total allowance is reached, you will no longer have access to hotspot data until your next monthly cycle. Once you add this perk, you’ll be charged today and then monthly on your payment cycle date. No carryover data. One purchase per line per billing cycle. Remove this perk anytime.

Unlimited Data

​ Enjoy unlimited data on America's
most reliable 5G network
for only $10/mo. An individual’s user experience will vary depending upon many factors, including the network (5G / 4G LTE, or All Access Home Internet) the customer is using and the device in use. We may prioritize your data behind other traffic. If the cell site you are connected to begins experiencing high demand during the duration of your session, your data speeds may be slower than the other traffic’s. Once the demand on the site lessens, or if you connect to a different site not experiencing high demand, your speed will return to normal. Any such network management practices will be disclosed in the descriptions of impacted plans. Remove this perk anytime.

Verizon Cloud

​ Protect your pictures, videos
and more with Verizon secure
cloud storage. You can purchase the Verizon Cloud from the My Verizon App. You’ll be charged $5 for 500 GB or $10 for 1 TB each month on your renewal date. You can remove this perk anytime. If you decide to remove Verizon Cloud, you’ll no longer have access to the service, including most of the content and files you have stored. If you decide to subscribe again within 30 days of canceling, you can restore your old content by calling us. After 30 days, your content will be permanently deleted.

Verizon Protect (Basic)

​ An all-in-one privacy and security
app that keeps your personal
information safe. An all-in-one privacy and security app that keeps your personal information safe. It’s your online world. Keep it that way. The Verizon Protect app gives you security and privacy for free. Protect your device from online threats and avoid unprotected networks, risky websites and dangerous downloads. For a detailed list of features, visit the Verizon Protect page. Verizon Protect is included on most Android devices. Download the app if it’s not included on your device. Remove this perk anytime.

Block Messaging

​ Block sending and receiving text,
picture and video messages. Block your phone from sending or receiving text messages, picture or video messages. This perk will also turn off the account balance display on the main screen of your phone. Remove this perk anytime.

Block Caller ID

​ Block your number on Caller ID
displays for outgoing calls. Block Caller ID will prevent your phone number from appearing on all of your outgoing calls, excluding those to 911 or toll-free numbers. Private, Anonymous or Restricted will appear on the receiver’s Caller ID readout. To block Caller ID on a specific outgoing call, press *67 before you dial. You can remove this perk anytime.
