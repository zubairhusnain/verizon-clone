What is mobile data and what is an unlimited data plan?

Simply put, mobile data is the digital information sent and received by your device over a wireless network. To establish this connection, your phone constantly searches for nearby cellular towers to transmit and receive an internet signal. This allows you to browse the web, stream music, and use apps even when you aren't connected to Wi-Fi. Understanding what cellular data is, is key to managing your monthly usage, as it powers everything from GPS navigation to checking your email on the go.

If you've ever wondered what data is in mobile phones, it's helpful to think of it as the fuel for your smartphone's online features. You can learn more about how this differs from home internet by exploring the difference between Wi-Fi and cellular data.

When selecting a cell phone data plan that aligns with your needs, what does unlimited data plan mean? An unlimited data plan is exactly what it sounds like: a plan that allows you to use as much cellular data as you need without worrying about overage charges.
